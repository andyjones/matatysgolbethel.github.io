Llywodraethwyr / Governors

Mae modd i rieni i ofyn am hyd at 3 chyfarfod gyda Chorff Llywodraethu mewn unrhyw flwyddyn ysgol, i drafod materion sy’n peri pryder iddynt. Bydd rhaid bodloni 4 gofyniad:
Bydd angen i rieni gyflwyno deiseb o blaid cynnal cyfarfod.

  • Bydd angen i rieni o leiaf 10% o ddisgyblion cofrestredig yr ysgol / 30 o ddisgyblion cofrestredig lofnodi’r ddeiseb.
  • Rhaid galw’r cyfarfod i drafod materion sy’n effeithio ar yr ysgol.
  • Ceir cynnal uchafswm o 3 chyfarfod yn ystod y flwyddyn ysgol.
  • Rhaid bod o leiaf 25 o ddiwrnodau ysgol ar ôl yn y flwyddyn ysgol.

Parents mat request up to 3 meetings with the Governing Body in any school year, to discuss issues that concern them. 4 requirements must be met:
Parents will need to submit a petition in favor of holding a meeting.

  • Parents of at least 10% of the registered pupils at the school / 30 pupils must sign the petition.
  • The meeting must be called to discuss issues affecting the school.
  • A maximum of 3 meetings may be conducted during the school year.
  • There must be at least 25 school days left in the school year.
