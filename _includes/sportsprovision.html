Nodau a Darpariaeth Chwaraeon

  • Lluniwyd Cynllun Gweithredol yn cynnwys amserlen blwyddyn o fodiwlau i ymateb i ofynion y Cwricwlwm Cenedlaethol.
  • Derbynia’r disgyblion o leiaf un wers Addysg Gorfforol yr wythnos lle datblygir medrau cyffredinol. Yn ogystal, bydd pob dosbarth, yn ei dro, yn derbyn sesiynau hanner tymor o hyfforddiant gymnasteg yng Nghanolfan Hamdden Arfon.
  • Cymera’r disgyblion yr ysgol rhan mewn twrnamentau lleol, (e.e. peldroed, rygbi,pêl-rwyd, criced, tennis, gymnasteg a.y.b. Mae tîm yn cystadlu yng Ngala Nofio Canolfan Hamdden Arfon.
  • Trefnir twrnamentau rhwng ysgolion cyfagos.
  • Mae timau yn chwarae gemau amrywiol.
  • Cynhelir Clybiau Chwaraeon ar ôl ysgol yn achlysurol i blant yr Adran Iau.

Gofynnir i’r plant ddod â gwisg addysg gorfforol addas, ar gyfer y gweithgareddau hyn. Cliciwch yma am fwy o wythoniaeth

Aims and Provisions for Sports

  • A Scheme of Work has been prepared for Physical Education, which includes a timetable of modules for the year in compliance with the requirements of the National Curriculum.
  • The pupils receive at least one Physical Education lesson a week in which basic skills are developed. In addition, each class in the junior section, in turn, will receive half-termly gymnastic lessons at the Arfon Leisure Centre.
  • The pupils participate in local sports tournaments,( e.g. football, rugby, netball, cricket, tennis, gymnastics etc) A team also competes in the Arfon Leisure Centre Swimming Gala.
  • Tournaments are arranged between neighbouring schools.
  • A weekly after school sports club is help for Year 2.
  • Sports clubs areoccasionally held for the Junior Section after school.
