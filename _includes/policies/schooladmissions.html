Polisi Derbyn yr Ysgol / The School's Admission Policy

Yn llawn amser ym mis Medi yn dilyn eu penblwydd yn 4 oed.
Yn rhan amser (bore / prynhawn) ym mis Medi yn dilyn eu penblwydd yn 3 oed.
Rhaid i bob disgybl wneud cais i’r AALl am le yn yr ysgol. Rhaid i ddisgyblion y dosbarth Meithrin hefyd wneud cais i’r AALl am fynediad i’r dosbarth Derbyn.
Full time in the September following their 4th birthday.
Part time (mornings / afternoons) in the September following their 3rd birthday.
All pupils must make a request to the LEA for a place in the school. Pupils who attend the Nursery class must also make a request to the LEA for entry to the Reception class.
