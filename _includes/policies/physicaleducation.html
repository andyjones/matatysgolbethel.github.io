Addysg Gorfforol / Physical Education

Mae'r ysgol, mewn partneriaeth â'r cartref, yn gyfrifol am ddatblygiad corfforol pob plentyn, yn ogystal â'i ddatblygiad addysgol a moesol. Felly, cynhelir gwersi Addysg Gorfforol o leiaf unwaith yr wythnos i adrannau'r Babanod ac Iau yng nghampfa'r ysgol, ar y buarthau, ar y cae, yn y pwll nofio yn y Ganolfan Hamdden neu yn y ganolfan tenis. Bydd mangre'r gwersi'n dibynnu ar ba uned o waith bydd y dosbarth yn ymdrin ag ar y tywydd.

Ein nod yw datblygu cryfder, rheolaeth ac esthetrwydd y plant trwy eu cael i fod yn gorfforol weithgar. Byddent hefyd yn cael cyfleoedd i weithio'n annibynnol a chydweithio ag eraill ac i lunio a rheoli gemau eu hunain.

Yn ddibynnol ar y fangre, disgwylir i'r plant wisgo dillad priodol ar gyfer y gwersi. Golyga hyn newid cyflawn o'u gwisg uchaf h.y. pâr o siortiau chwaraeon plaen (dim denim) a chrys T chwaraeon gwyn (nid festiau). Byddant angen treinyrs ar gyfer gweithgareddau ar y buarthau / cae. Ni chaniateir gwisgo unrhyw bympiau ar gyfer gweithgraeddau yn y neuadd ag eithrio’r plant sydd â chlefyd ar eu traed.

Ni chaniateir gwisgo modrwyau, clustlysau nac oriawr mewn unrhyw wers Addysg Gorfforol, (gwerthfawrogir petai’r plant yn eu gadael adref ar ddyddiau Addysg Gorfforol).


Os oes unrhyw reswm meddygol pam na all plentyn gymryd rhan mewn gwersi Addysg Gorfforol, gofynnir yn garedig i'r rhieni hysbysu'r ysgol yn ysgrifenedig.

Yn ddibynnol ar y tywydd, ymdrechir trefnu gemau rygbi, peldroed neu bêl-rwyd gyda ysgolion eraill, mynychu twrnamentau yng Nghanolfannau Hamdden Arfon a mynychu gala nofio Ysgolion Arfon.
The school, in partnership with the home, is responsible for every child's physical development as well as his/her educational and moral development. Consequently, Physical Education lessons for the Infant and Junior Classes are held at least once a week in the school hall, the field, the schoolyard, the swimming pool at the Leisure Centre or the Tennis Centre. The venue for the lessons will depend on which unit the class will be following and upon the weather.

Our aim is to develop strength, control and an aesthetic appreciation by getting the children physically active. They will also have opportunities to work independently and with others and to form and regulate their own games.

Depending on the venue, the children are expected to wear appropriate clothing for each lesson. This means a complete set of outer-wear, i.e. a pair of plain sports shorts (not denim) and a white sports T-shirt (not vest tops). Trainers will be needed for activities on the yard/ field. The wearing of pumps is not permitted for activities in the school hall except when children have a foot infection.

The wearing of rings, earrings and watches will not be allowed in any Physical Education lessons, (it would be appreciated if the children left them at home on Physical Education days).

If children are unable to participate because of illness or injury, parents are kindly requested to inform the class teacher in writing.

Depending on the weather, we will attempt to arrange rugby, football or netball games against other schools in the area, participate in tournaments in the Arfon Leisure Centre and to participate in the swimming gala for Arfon Schools.
