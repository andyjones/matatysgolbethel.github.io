Diogelwch Yn Yr Haul / Safety In The Sun

Yn ystod tywydd cynnes, fe’ch anogir i roi eli haul ar eich plentyn yn y bore ac i roi cyflenwad o’r eli yn ei fag iddo ei roi cyn mynd allan i chwarae.
Gofynnir i chi sicrhau bod gan eich plentyn gap i’w wisgo ar dywydd braf.
During warm weather you are advised to apply sun cream on your child in the morning and to put a supply of the cream in his bag so that he may apply it before playtime.
We ask you to ensure that your child has a hat to wear during warm weather.
