Addysg Rhyw

Mae’r ysgol wedi ymateb i ofynion Cynulliad Cenedlaethol Cymru i weithredu rhaglen ‘Addysg Rhyw a Pherthnasoedd’. Defnyddir fframwaith ABCh a’r Cwricwlwm Gwyddoniaeth fel sylfaen. Y nod yw meithrin hunan barch a deallusrwydd bechgyn a genethod o’u cyrff eu hunain drwy ymdrîn â materion rhywiol mewn ffordd sensitif a chan feithrin agwedd iach ac aeddfed tuag at brosesau bywyd a materion rhywiol.

Ymdrechir i ateb unrhyw gwestiynau sydd yn codi’n naturiol gan y plant ac fe ymdrinnir â'r maes mewn modd sensitif a synhwyrol gan bwysleisio’r perthynas teuluol a chariadus. Mae hawl gan rieni i eithrio’u plant o rannau o’r gwersi Addysg Rhyw. Gallech, pe dymunwch, ymgynghori â'r Pennaeth ynghylch polisi’r ysgol ar Addysg Rhyw.

Sex Education

The school has reacted to the Welsh Assembly’s requirement to provide a ‘Sex and Relationships’ education program, the basis of which is the PSE Framework and the Science Curriculum. The aim is to develop boys and girls’ self-respect and understanding of their own bodies by dealing with aspects of a sexual nature in a sensitive manner and by developing a healthy and mature attitude towards life processes and sexual matters.

Any questions that may naturally arise will be answered appropriately. All matters will be dealt with in a sensitive and sensible manner with emphasis on loving and family relationships. Parents may remove their child from some of the Sex Education lessons. You may, should you wish, consult the Headteacher concerning the school’s policy on Sex Education.
