Cyswllt a Rhieni / Links with Parents

Gweithredir polisi 'drws agored' yn yr ysgol lle mae rhyddid a chroeso i rieni, drwy drefnu apwyntiad ymlaen llaw, i ymweld â'r ysgol i drafod unrhyw agwedd ar addysg, lles neu hapusrwydd eu plant. Rydym i gyd yn cydweithio er lles pob plentyn yn yr ysgol.
Gwahoddir rhieni i'r ysgol i weld a thrafod gwaith a datblygiad eu plant ddwywaith y flwyddyn, yn nhymor yr Hydref a thymor yr Haf. Hefyd gwahoddir rhieni a chyfeillion yr ysgol i fynychu cyngherddau, a.y.b. i wylio’r plant yn perfformio. Gwahoddir rhieni i wasanaeth diwedd blwyddyn yn flynyddol.

The school follows an 'open door' policy where any parent is welcome, through prior arrangement, to visit the school to discuss any matter relating to their child's education, welfare or happiness. We all work together for the benefit of each and every child at the school.
Parents are invited to the school to see and discuss their child's work and development twice a year, in the Autumn and Summer terms. Parents and friends of the school are also invited to attend concerts etc. to watch the children perform. Parents are invited to an annual end of year service.
