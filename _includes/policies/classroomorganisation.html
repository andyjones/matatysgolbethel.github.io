Trefniadaeth Dosbarthiadau / Classroom Organisation

Trefnir yr ysgol yn 6 dosbarth gan sicrhau cydbwysedd o ran niferoedd a chan geisio osgoi dosbarthiadau gyda mwy na dwy flynedd o ystod oedran.
Pan fo rhaniadau blwyddyn yn anorfod, gwneir hyn ar sail oedran.

The school is arranged into 6 classes with the aim of securing a balance in terms of numbers and creating units where the age range is limited to no more than two years.
When splitting a year group is inevitable, this is done on the basis of age.
The objective is to arrange classes to ensure a lively mutual reaction both verbally and in the written work between the teacher and the child, between the teacher and the whole class and between the child and his peers.
A full time teacher and nursery assistant have been appointed to each class in the Foundation Phase section.
