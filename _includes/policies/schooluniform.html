Gwisg Ysgol

Mae gan yr ysgol hon wisg ysgol swyddogol. Mae'r wisg yn cynnwys crys polo glas } golau/crys t, crys chwys glas tywyll/hwdi, sgert neu drowsus llwyd/du, (dim trowsusau chwaraeon na jîns) ac esgidiau neu drainyrs addas. Ni chaniateir sodlau na fflipfflops.

Yn yr Haf caniateir gwisgo trowsus byr neu dri-chwarter llwyd/du neu ffrog las a gwyn (gingham) i'r genethod.
Disgwylir i'r plant fod wedi eu gwisgo'n lân a thrwsiadus, a bod eu dillad yn cydymffurfio â’r wisg ysgol. Gofynnwn yn garedig am eich cefnogaeth gyda'r wisg ysgol.
Gellir archebu crysau polo/crysau t a chrysau chwys/hwdis drwy’r ysgol unwaith y tymor.

Cyfrifoldeb rhieni yw sicrhau bod enw eu plentyn wedi ei bwytho/ysgrifennu ar bob dilledyn y mae’n ei wisgo neu’n newid iddo fel rhan o weithgareddau’r ysgol.

Ni chaniateir gwisgo clustlysau yn yr ysgol ag eithrio 'stỳds'.

School Uniform

This school has an official uniform. The uniform includes a light blue polo shirt/t-shirt, a dark blue sweatshirt/hoodie, grey/black skirt or trousers, (not sports trousers or jeans), and suitable shoes or trainers. Heels and flip-flops are not permitted.

In the summer the children can wear grey/ black shorts/three quarter length trousers or a blue and white gingham dress for the girls.
The children are expected to be neatly dressed in clothes, which conform to the school uniform. We respectfully request your co-operation with the school uniform. Polo/T shirts and sweatshirts/hoodies may be ordered through the school once a term.

It is the parent’s responsibility to ensure that their child's name is sewn/written on each piece of clothing worn or changed into as part of the school's activities.

The wearing of earrings is not permitted in school, with the exception of 'studs'
