Every child has the right to a free education and this is the case in Ysgol Bethel. However, it would not be possible to offer some activities without your support and co-operation. We kindly request a contribution towards transportation costs to swimming lessons and for educational visits. This is voluntary. Also, following guidelines set out by the Education Authority, we ask for a part payment for musical instrument lessons.
